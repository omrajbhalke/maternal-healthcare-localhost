Maternal Health Risk Prediction using ML

About the Project

Maternal health is a vital concern, and early risk detection can make a life-saving difference. Our project uses machine learning to determine whether a pregnant individual is Healthy or Risky based on key maternal health indicators.

By analyzing factors such as age, blood pressure, blood sugar level, body temperature, and heart rate, our model delivers fast and accurate predictions. This helps healthcare providers and expecting mothers make timely, informed decisions.

Our goal is to enhance prenatal care through AI, enabling proactive monitoring and promoting safer pregnancies with clear, data-driven health insights.
